Content

OG FORTNITE ARSENAL

Welcome to OG Fortnite Arsenal, your ultimate guide to the iconic weapons and items that have shaped the Fortnite landscape since its inception. As a passionate Fortnite enthusiast, I also miss the days of when Fortnite first released back in 2018, and how we would hop on with the boys after school and catch some dubs. I created this website to cater to your nostalgic yearnings by delving into the OG weapons and items that have become legendary within the game. OG Fortnite Arsenal is not just a website; it's a journey down memory lane. Whether you're a seasoned Fortnite veteran or a newcomer eager to learn about the game's roots, my website brings back memories of the early days when each weapon and item held a special place in players' hearts. Dive deep into the profiles of OG weapons and items. From the humble beginnings of the Tactical Shotgun to the game-changing introduction of the Boogie Bomb, I've got you covered. Explore their stats, rarity, and the strategies that made them a fan favorite. Each weapon and item profile includes detailed descriptions, helping both newcomers and seasoned players understand the unique characteristics that define OG Fortnite.

OG WEAPONS

In the realm of OG Fortnite Weapons, each firearm and close-quarter companion tells a story, a tale of the early days when Tomatoe Town was still just Tomatoe Town. From the thunderous boom of the OG Pump Shotgun to the precision of the Bolt-Action Sniper Rifle, this page is a tribute to the iconic arsenal that shaped Fortnite's history.

OG ITEMS

Welcome to the OG Items repository. From healing essentials like the Chug Jug to strategic assets like the Port-a-Fort, you can read and learn about all of these. Whether you're a veteran looking to reminisce or a newcomer eager to understand Fortnite's foundations, this page is your guide to the iconic items from Season 1 of Fortnite!
